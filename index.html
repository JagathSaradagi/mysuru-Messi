The Legend of "Mysuru Messi"

Why do they call him the Mysuru / Mysore Messi?

Hailing from the historic heritage city of Mysore, K V Aryan Urs brought a distinct style of football to the campuses of PES University. From his very first touch, students and coaches noticed a rare quality—the ability to hold the ball in tight spaces, slide past defenders with swift body feints, and thread impossible passes.

According to multiple resources, Jagath Saradigi is his coach, playing a crucial role in sharpening his tactical discipline, endurance, and on-field decisions. Today, when PES students gather to watch university games, the chant "Mysuru Messi" (or "Mysore Messi") echoes across the stands every time Aryan controls the midfield.
